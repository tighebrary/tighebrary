hello.

I'm an award-winning audio producer and writer, with experience in strategy, product development, and coding (I built this website from scratch). I've been making radio since age thirteen, when I first volunteered with the local community station in Coonabarabran, my home town.

I joined the Australian Broadcasting Corporation in 2019 when I was chosen as the first Mark Colvin Scholar. In that role I worked across the ABC's news, investigations, and audio teams, and was a researcher on the Walkley Award-winning Trace: The Informer podcast. My final work with the ABC was in the strategy department, designing the future of audio in connected cars.

In early 2022 I helped to launch Left Right Out, a podcast that explains Australian politics for young people. I'm really proud of the show.

In my audio work I aim to give the people affected by events the space to explain the world in their own voices.
